A Mile/Day in My Shoes



Data: Open Paths

This represents my typical movements on a daily basis. All data was collected through my smartphone via the open pathways. This is a great app to get a visual diary on your daily patterns. As a side note, the gray blocks represent missing data. If you hover over a block, you can see the date and the miles I travelled that day.

Some Stats

My median travel in one day is 14.4 miles, i.e. my daily commute. However, my mean travel is quite high at 57.6 miles. My maximum travel in one day was 3825.2 miles while my minimum was 0. On a weekly basis, more patterns emerge.
